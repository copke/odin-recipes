Tuna Fish Salad

This tuna fish is excellent served on top of a green salad or between two pieces of bread with fresh lettuce. Sprinkle a little paprika on top to add a little flavor and color.

Tuna Fish Salad Ingredients

  • 1(5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, parsley, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Season with paprika; refrigerate until chilled.
